Success criteria
  • Read 120–150 wpm ≥90% accuracy

Educational journey
  1. Entry: KWL + short reading screen places research groups.
  2. Weeks 1–2: question generation; note‑taking practice; first draft paragraph.
  3. Weeks 3–4: synthesize notes; revise with checklists; create visuals.
  4. Weeks 5–6: rehearsal cycles; peer feedback and self‑assessment.
  5. Weeks 7–8: gallery walk showcase; reflection + next goals.
  6. Next: Discover 3 (SKY 1) for multi‑paragraph reports.

  • Feedback linked to success criteria
