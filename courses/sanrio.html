Sanrio Grade 6

A focused writing workshop that marries explicit grammar with genre‑based instruction.

Open Champion English Grammar & Writing G6 6/12 • left 6 210.000₫/session
Big ideas
  • Clarity & Structure
  • Purpose & Audience
  • Evidence & Reasoning
Essential questions
  • How do writers make readers care?
  • How does grammar shape meaning?
  • What evidence convinces a reader?
Intended outcomes
  • Tense control across present/past/future
  • Sentence variety: compound/complex
  • Academic vocabulary & cohesion
  • Task‑type writing
Success criteria
  • ≥85% grammar accuracy in edits
  • 2–3 paragraphs (180–220 words) with thesis + support
  • 2‑minute evidence‑based speech
Educational journey
  1. Entry: cold write + grammar quick‑check sets focus goals.
  2. Cycle A: narrative techniques; sentence variety drills; publish one story.
  3. Cycle B: explanation → argument; evidence and cohesion; publish two essays.
  4. Publication week: select best piece; peer edit; public reading.
  5. Next: IELTS foundation or advanced writing seminar.
How we teach
Evidence‑based practices
  • SRSD plan‑write‑revise‑edit
  • Sentence combining/expansion
  • Deliberate practice with feedback cycles
  • Rubric‑based conferencing
INTASC alignment
1245678
INTASC connections (course‑specific)
  • Std 4 – Content Knowledge: Clauses, cohesion and genre features taught with mentor texts and color‑coding.
  • Std 5 – Application of Content: Argument and explanation tasks tied to science/history topics.
  • Std 6 – Assessment: Cold writes at start/end; analytic rubrics for ideas, organization and conventions guide conferencing.
  • Std 7 – Planning: Backward‑mapped units publish six polished pieces per term; mini‑lessons respond to data.
  • Std 8 – Instructional Strategies: Mini‑lessons, conferring and targeted revision checklists with exemplars.
  • Std 2 – Learner Differences: Choice of prompts; scaffolded frames and vocabulary banks for multilingual learners.
