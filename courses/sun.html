Big ideas

Essential questions
  • How do I support a position logically?

Intended outcomes
  • Weekly Speaking Parts 1‑3 drills

Success criteria
  • Target Band 5.5–7.0; +0.5 within 12–16 weeks

Educational journey

  2. Weeks 1–4: remedy weakest sub‑skill; timed micro‑tasks each session; vocabulary notebook routines.
  3. Weeks 5–8: integrate skills; two partial mocks with oral and written feedback.
  4. Weeks 9–12: two full mocks; targeted drills; exam booking guidance.
  5. Post‑course: maintenance plan and recommended mock cadence.
